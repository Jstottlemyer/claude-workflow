Persona Metrics — Diagrams Preview v2 · 4 new variants

Feedback this round addresses

  • D-D layout is the favorite (light theme, blueprint aesthetic).
  • Judge nodes were too big in D-D — tighten them.
  • Brighter colors / lines from D-B — Persona Metrics deserves a real accent color, not just another grey side node.
  • JS2 (the /plan Judge) doesn't record metrics — must be visually distinguished from JS1 and JS3 so the asymmetry is legible.
  • Gates could expand to include agent personas (even though they aren't 100% fixed). At least one variant should explore this.

Previous round (D-A through D-D) backed up at diagrams-preview-v1.html.

Intent: Each multi-agent gate (SR, P, C) shows its actual personas in the sub-text — answers the "what's running here?" question without exploding the gate into a subgraph. Color-tinted gates (warm for review, violet for plan, rose for check) so they're identifiable; non-multi-agent stages (K/S/B/W) stay neutral blueprint.
