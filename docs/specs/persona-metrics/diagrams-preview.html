Tweak knobs (just in case)

If something's off, the most likely tweaks:

  • JS2 ghost too subtle / too loud — adjust synthGhost classDef (fill / stroke contrast).
  • Persona lists too long — wrap differently, or drop sub-text for one stage and keep for others.
  • Pastel fills too washed out on your monitor — bump fills one shade darker (e.g. #dbeafe → #bfdbfe for setup).
  • Edge labels too crowded around PM — drop "records" labels and let the dotted/solid distinction carry the meaning.
  • "surfaces drift" label phrasing — alternatives: "reads at /wrap-insights", "drift render".
